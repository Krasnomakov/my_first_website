Outcome #2

Personal Portfolio

PROFESSIONAl SKILLS: personal leadership

TECHNICAL SKILLS: trends & development

This website is part of my studies @ Fontys ICT & Media Design. I made it simple and practical in order to be able to learn how to apply different visual effects. For me it is important to make the website visaully appealing to the user. Exploring modern trends in UI design I found out that animation and scroll animation are strong trends. However, most of it uses Javascript and I don't know it well enough to understand the code.

I began building websites using material design as inspiration, and received positive feedback from students and teachers. I then discovered neumorphism, skeumorphism, and glassmorphism as design trends, but found that they required more elements and styling to look nice on simple websites. I decided to go with a pixelated retro game style instead, as it is popular and entertaining for users who enjoy retro games.

After several tries I managed to combine different aniamtion and style effects that I liked with a simplicity of 8 bit style. I used artificial intelligence text 2 image service NightCafe to produce appropriate images for portfolio highlights and projects. I managed to make the images for portfolio in a similar color scheme and they natively fitted into a website.

With this portfolio I want to deliver my vision for minimalistic design and implementation of latest technology such as different Pure CSS effects and Artificial Intelligence.
