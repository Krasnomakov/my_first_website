ChatGPT Experience

Career Choice advice with ChatGPT

In this experiment I utilized ChatGPT to give a career advice relevant to my personal situation.

Firstly, I trained it on my data by supplying information about myself. Then I started to ask questions and filtering the data and ranking professions based on answers.

Eventually I managed to get a list of 10 professions that I will be able to take based on my education.

When asked about the role of ChatGPT and possible jobs using it the AI gave lessons, tutorials and other necessary information to train relevant skills for jobs related to ChatGPT.
